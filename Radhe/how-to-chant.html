üìø How to Chant Radha Naam

A complete step-by-step guide to begin your spiritual journey

1

Prepare Your Sacred Space

Creating the right environment is essential for deep meditation and connection.

  • Find a quiet, clean place where you won't be disturbed for at least 10-15 minutes
  • Sit comfortably with your back straight - you can use a chair, cushion, or meditation mat
  • Face East or North direction if possible (traditional practice)
  • Keep a picture or idol of Radha-Krishna in front of you (optional but recommended)
  • Ensure good ventilation and comfortable temperature

üí° Pro Tip

Keep a dedicated space for your daily practice. Your energy will build up in this space, making it easier to meditate each day.

2

Set Your Sacred Intention

Before beginning, connect with your heart and dedicate your practice.

  • Close your eyes and take 3 deep breaths to center yourself
  • Offer your jaap to Radha and Krishna with pure devotion
  • Set an intention: "May this practice purify my heart and bring me closer to divine love"
  • Light a diya (lamp) or incense if you wish - this purifies the atmosphere
  • You can also offer flowers or water to the deity

ü™î Spiritual Insight

The power of your jaap multiplies when done with sincere devotion and clear intention. Quality matters more than quantity.

3

Begin Your Chanting

There are three traditional ways to chant, each with its own benefits:

üîä Vaikhari (Aloud Chanting)

Chant loudly enough to hear yourself clearly. Best for beginners as it helps maintain focus and prevents mind from wandering.

ü§´ Upanshu (Whispered)

Move your lips slightly but sound is barely audible. Brings deeper concentration and is more powerful than aloud chanting.

üßò Manasik (Mental)

Pure mental repetition without any sound or lip movement. Most powerful form - achieved after practice. Creates deepest meditation state.

Complete with Gratitude

How you end your practice is as important as how you begin.

  • After 108 chants, sit quietly for 1-2 minutes with eyes closed
  • Feel the divine energy flowing through your body
  • Notice the peace and stillness in your mind
